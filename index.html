About Us

FC STRIKERS is a street football team in Sharjah,UAE. Consisting of 20 players. We have been participating in different tournaments since 2017.

Our Vision

"Often skillful players don't get a chance to showcase their skills. We are on a mission to change that. We provide these players with an opportunity to be a part of a team filled with like-minded skillful players and showcase their skills. "
