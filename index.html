About Us

FC STRIKERS is a street football team located in Sharjah,UAE. comprising of 20 skilled players. The team has actively participated in various local tournaments since its inception in 2017.

Our Mission

"Many skillful players are often overlooked and unable to display their abilities. Our mission is to address this issue by offering these players an opportunity to join a team of like-minded individuals and exhibit their talents to a wider audience."
